Giordano Rogers

1985 - 2026

IN LOVING MEMORY

It is with heavy hearts that we announce the sudden passing of our beloved son, brother, friend, and visionary Giordano Rogers on March 21, 2026.

Giordano was a self-taught innovator who began his journey in technology with a free web design course, discovering a passion for building digital tools that could change lives. Though he once stepped away from college, the encouragement of his father—who saw his dedication studying in the library—brought him back. He enrolled at Bunker Hill Community College with fierce determination, aiming for a perfect 4.0 GPA and transfer to a top program. His path led him to data science, machine learning, and ultimately acceptance as a proud 2024 Hack.Diversity Fellow, where he thrived among peers committed to breaking barriers in tech.

Driven by a deep sense of duty during what he called one of the most critical moments in history, Giordano dreamed of creating software that amplified human potential—applications in artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and social media that could bring people closer to themselves, raise collective intelligence, and foster value, meaning, and joy amid rapid cultural change. He was motivated to be a better human being because he believed actions were profoundly consequential, and he dedicated himself to building resources that helped others become their best selves. Committed to providing for his family and enriching lives, Giordano's legacy is one of transformation, creativity, and unwavering optimism in the power of technology to improve the world.

Giordano passed away peacefully on March 21, 2026. He will be deeply missed but never forgotten. Rest in power—your vision lives on.
